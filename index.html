My Services

  • Website Development

    We will work together to plan your ideal website, then leave the rest to me. After your website is finished and you are happy, I will help you deploy your website on your preferred hosting service, or I can recommend my favorite.

  • Responsive Web Design

    Today, around 60% of website visits are done from mobile devices. It is essential that your website works on smaller resolutions. I make sure my designs scale properly on all popular screen sizes.

  • Walking with you

    Communication is key. You will be updated frequently on the progress of your website. After your website is finished, I can help you maintain it longterm if you need to make regular updates.

  • Logo Design

    A logo for your business helps bring a professional touch to your website. I will work with you to create a simple and elegant logo in addition to making your website.
